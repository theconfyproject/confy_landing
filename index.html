Match

Finding the best suited applicants.

Through a custom made matching process we find the best suited candidates for each conference.

Connect

Connecting organisers and aspiring volunteers.

Simplifying and speeding up the selection process by connecting the right candidates with the events in need of staff.

Community

Building a community of techies from all walks of life.

Whether you are a student, an IT specialist or a professional considering a career change into the field of tech, here you can meet likeminded people, build your network and harness the power of the community.

How it will work

Create your profile

Create a profile and choose which event you would like to participate in by selecting between technologies, locations and topics.

Apply to events

Fill in the application form of the selected event and let the organisers get to know you by answering their questionnaire.

Become a tech volunteer

Become a tech conference volunteer and have a great time with awesome people while learning and networking.
